Sustainable Cities & Communities (SDG 11)

TransitSight directly advances SDG 11 by promoting safer and more efficient public transit usage. By eliminating commuter guesswork and reducing overcrowding discomfort, we encourage higher ridership — fundamentally reducing urban carbon footprints and traffic congestion.
